About Us

Get to know the story behind Fast Food

Our Story

Fast Food started in 2022 as a small kitchen in Nairobi with one goal: get good food to people fast, without cutting corners on quality.

What began as a single delivery bike has grown into a team that serves hundreds of customers across the city every single day. We still cook every order fresh, just like we did on day one.

Our Values

Speed

We respect your time. Your order reaches you in 30 minutes or less.

Freshness

Nothing is pre-made. Every meal is cooked once you order it.

Honesty

What you see on the menu is exactly what lands on your plate.
